SIX BASIC QUESTION YOU MUST
ASK BEFORE BUYING ANY LAND

Real Estate Investment requires huge of capital outlay. any losse incuured in any form is a huge loss. You should never be a victim to a real estate scam. Sometimes, its not even a delibrate action of an a seller to defraud the investor of his money, it could be that there are certain factors that both sellers and buyers are ignorant of In cases of mistake, the buyer is at the loosing end.

I have prepared 6 basic questions that you must ask and ensure you get the answers before paying for land in any location in Enugu.


1. What is the dedicated Land use: Different Land are mapped for different land uses. You have to make sure that the dedicated land use corresponds with your intended land use.


2. What is the nature of the land: Does the topography suite your use? Is it swampy or dry land?


3. Is the land committed by the government or Free?


4. Is the Land Revocked: This has been so common in Enugu in the past few years and should not be ignored. Any land can be revocked for overriding but it is a different ball game if you buy an already revocked land.


5. Is there community/family Dispute in the layout?


6. What titles are enough proof of ownership? In some communities you will need alocation paper,land receipt from power of attorney committee, deeds of assignement, power of attorney amd Survey plan.

It is mostly difficult to get all this information. It therefore becomes pertinent that you work with professionals. Let me be your real estate plug.
